# Privacy Policy for RamyaChat 💬✨
**Provider: RatoLab**

[日本語版はこちら (Japanese Version)](./PRIVACY_POLICY_ja.md)

RamyaChat ("the App") values your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect and how we use it to provide a seamless messaging experience.

## 1. Information We Collect
To provide and maintain our services, we collect the following data:

* **Account Data**: Email address and username (managed securely via Firebase Authentication).
* **Profile Data**: Display name and user-uploaded avatars.
* **Content Data**: Chat messages and friend lists.
* **Technical Data**: Push notification tokens and basic device information.

## 2. Purpose of Use
The collected information is used solely for:

* User authentication and secure account management.
* Delivering real-time chat messages and notifications.
* Improving service quality and enhancing security.

## 3. Third-Party Services
We utilize trusted third-party providers:

* **Google Firebase**: For Authentication and Firestore database management.
* **Expo**: For application infrastructure and push notification delivery.

## 4. Contact Us
For any inquiries regarding this policy, please contact **RatoLab (TomatoJuice)**.
